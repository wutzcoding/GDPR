AI FLOW SOLUTIONS' PRIVACY AND PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION POLICY

Introduction

On May 25, 2018, the new General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) came into effect, establishing the rules applicable to the processing of personal data by companies and public entities with the goal of ensuring the protection of the aforementioned data.

Data Collection and Processing Responsibility

With knowledge of the GDPR, AI Flow Solutions adopts administrative and technological measures to comply. Thus, AI Flow Solutions is qualified as responsible for the collection and processing of personal data whenever it determines the purposes and means of processing, ensuring compliance with all provisions of the GDPR and other applicable data protection and privacy legislation.

What do we collect?

Personal Data we consider:

  • Identification data, namely Name, Age, Tax Identification Number, Citizen Card Number/Identity Card Number, Social Security Number, and Address.
  • Contact data, such as Telephone contact or email address.
  • Banking data.
  • Among other data necessary for the provision of our service/product.

How do we collect data?

The Personal Data we collect depends on the context of interactions between clients, suppliers, or other interested parties with AI Flow Solutions. These interactions are made for various reasons through contact with the company, either in person or through telephone means or through our website or email.

How do we process the data?

The processing of personal data is a set of operations carried out on personal data, by automated or manual means. It includes the collection, registration, organization, structuring, conservation, adaptation or alteration, recovery, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or making available, comparison or interconnection, limitation, erasure, or destruction of personal data.

What rights do data subjects have?

In accordance with the terms of current legislation, the data subject has the right to exercise various prerogatives, including access, correction, deletion, restriction of use, portability, and opposition to the processing of their personal information. To exercise your rights, you should contact via email at info@aiflowsolutions.eu.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

AI Flow Solutions reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. If there are changes to this policy, the date of the last modification, visible at the footer, will be updated.
